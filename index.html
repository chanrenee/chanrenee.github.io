about us

Like many great stories, Hypnotic Monkey started with a beer. We were two college friends set out to make a beer thats strong, yet taste like a great IPA. After years of mastering the craft, we finally created it. Now we want you to try it, too. Based in Atlanta, Georgia, Hypnotic Monkey will shortly be opening up our own brewery. We prides ourselves on only using three main ingredients- so you never have to worry about what goes into your favorite beer.

Barley

To create the malt and sweet flavor that all IPAs have- and also to create the high alcohol content.

Hops

Although we love the sweetness of the barley, we use Hops to tone down the sweetness just a tad.

Pineapple

This is our "secret" ingredient. It is what makes Hypnotic Monkey taste so tangy and good. Try it yourself if you don't believe us!
